## ğŸ’¡ Key Insights & Business Recommendation
While analyzing the data, I discovered a significant behavioral pattern:
- **The "Student" Anomaly:** Students have the highest conversion rates for term deposits, but specifically during the months of **April-July** and **November**. 
- **The "Why":** This aligns perfectly with European summer breaks, graduation gifts, and pre-winter financial planning.
- **Actionable Strategy:** The bank can significantly optimize its telemarketing budget by launching targeted "Student Deposit Promos" exclusively during those peak months, while shifting focus to other professions (like management or blue-collar workers) for the rest of the year.
